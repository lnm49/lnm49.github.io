North America

North America is my home continent and as such is the one that i've discovered the most.

NA

New York, US

Nice place to go with family or friends, very busy city with a lot going on all the time. My favorite part is going to Central Park or ice skating at Rockefeller Center.

Maine, US

Went to summer camp here, a lot of natural parks and amazing lobster.

North Carolina, US

Visited the Biltmore Estate and enjoyed the drives on the woods through the mountains.

Washington, US

Capital of the U.S. and has a very nice college town called Georgetown.

Nassau, Bahamas

Going through the tropical island, the waters are clear blue. Very nice to go through the different islands and on the boat.

San Jose, Costa Rica

Some of the best surfing in the planet.

NA

Florida, US

Been living here most of my life, famous mostly for Miami, Disney, and places like the Keys.

Mexico City, Mexico

Amazing food and a lot of spices added to the meals.

Acapulco, Mexico

The sunsets from the top of the rocky island are amazing.

San Juan, Puerto Rico

They have a very different culture from most places in the U.S. even though still considered a part of it. The food is amazing and the people are very happy and energetic.

Boston, US

A city filled with students and colleges; it snows at certain times of year.

South America

Home place of my predecessors, a beautiful place full of flora, fauna and happy people.

SA

Buenos Aires, Argentina

Best steak I’ve ever had, they also have a huge passion for soccer.

Quito, Ecuador

They have volcanoes around the city’s capital.

Galapagos, Ecuador

Known for its vast wildlife and exotic animals you can find. I learned how to scuba dive here.

Cali, Colombia

They call this city the capital of salsa.

Cartagena, Colombia

A historic city that looks a lot like San Juan. Both the Spanish and the pirates fought there; you can even visit a castle called Castillo San Felipe.

SA

Lima, Peru

They have some of the best seafood in the planet.

Cusco (Machu Picchu), Peru

Machu Picchu is one of the seven wonders of the world. It’s at a very high altitude and represents a creation of the Incas.

Bogotá, Colombia

I lived here for a few years. The capital of Colombia, it’s a city at a high altitude surrounded by mountains.

Armenia, Colombia

Also called “La Zona Cafetera” they are known for their amazing coffee, one of the best in the world.

Europe

The old continent and the birthplace of the western culture, the richest of the continents in terms of culture and languages.

EU

Brussels, Belgium

It’s a relatively small and quiet city, I passed by on my way to Amsterdam. They had amazing chocolate shops and small local stores.

Paris, France

My favorite city in the planet. Known for its amazing history and culture that spans from the best wines and cheeses to the best-known art museums like the Louvre.

Budapest, Hungary

A country located in Eastern Europe, it has a lot of monuments and a very exotic culture. Eastern Europe tends to be much cheaper to visit than the Western side.

Madrid, Spain

The capital of Spain, known for its history and amazing architecture and museums. It also has amazing food including jamon iberico, croquetas, and its famous paella.

Barcelona, Spain

Although it’s one of the main cities in Spain, they are also a part of Cataluna and therefore speak both Spanish and Catalan as their main languages.

EU

Venice, Italy

A historic city with very special architecture, it uses gondolas as a way of transportation as there are canals going all through the city.

Luxembourg, Luxembourg

One of the richest countries in the world, also small in territory.

Amsterdam, Netherlands

Known for being a very liberal city, its economy is mainly focused on tourism. Like Venice, they also have canals throughout the city and people usually choose to bike.

London, United Kingdom

The financial capital of the world were most banks hold their HQ. The city as hundreds of years of history and has a monarchy.

Africa

A misterious land home of ancient and particular cultures, also the birthplace of human kind.

AF

Botswana

A country in Southern Africa, it has a lot of wildlife and is known to have the best safaris. It was my favorite country I visited while in my trip to Africa.

Kenia

A country in East Africa known for its landscapes and wildlife.

Mozambique

Located in South Africa, it has amazing beaches, and their main language is Portuguese.

Zambia

I visited one of the seven wonders of the world here, called Victoria Falls, it was an amazing experience to see the waterfalls while walking on brides through the forest.

AF

Johannesburg, South Africa

It’s the largest city in South Africa, has more of a city vibe than most places there as wildlife is more limited.

Cape Town, South Africa

Oldest city in South Africa, I also happened to go great white shark cage diving here. The city itself is beautiful and very different from anything I’ve seen.

Zimbabwe

Has a lot of natural parks and opportunities to go on safaris with amazing views.

Asia

The biggest and the most populated of the continents, with an exceptional sense of discipline and martiality.

AS

Istambul, Turkey

It’s located in both Asia and Europe, mostly in Asia. A lot of empires had a big influence on Istanbul as it has a geographical advantage of also having two seas.

AS

Cappadocia, Turkey

Has an interesting architecture as a lot of houses there are built inside of rocks and with rocks as materials for construction. They are well known for their hot air balloon tours which was an amazing experience.

Oceania

The old continent and the birthplace of the western culture, the richest of the continents in terms of culture and languages.

Fiji

A country in the South Pacific that is made of over 300 islands. I had the chance to go for two weeks on a mission trio for community service and it was a lifechanging experience.
